About Us

Founded in 2015, mydilse an online fundraising platform that connects communities providing a tool for students and teachers to formalize their fundraising efforts and a channel for funders to find students and teachers they wish to support.
Our team is young, diverse, and passionate about education. We believe that access to education should not be dependent on wealth.

Transparency & Efficiency

We know that you care about how effectively your donation is being used. That’s why we show you how every rupee was spent, how you made a difference, and how grateful the students and teachers are!

How it Works

To ensure integrity, we’re there through every step of every project. We vet all requests, purchase each item, and ship materials directly to verified schools using leading e-commerce platforms

You fund a project

Choose a project that inspires you and give any amount. You can give as little as rupees 100.

We fulfill the order

When a project is fully funded, we purchase all the requested items and ship them directly to the school using leading e-commerce platforms

Teachers thank you

Every donor gets a thank-you letter from the teacher, photos from the classroom, and a report of how each rupee was spent.
